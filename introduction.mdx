## Getting Started

CodinIT is an AI app development platform that enables users to build AI-powered applications with ease. With its open-source architecture, users can prototype and develop full-stack apps quickly, leveraging the flexibility to use any model and framework. CodinIT's platform is designed to be vendor-agnostic, allowing for seamless integration and deployment.
